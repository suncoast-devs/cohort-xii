# README

- App for tracking taco companies and their various locations

* Taco Company

  - Address (corporate)
  - Name
  - Year founded
  - Logo

* Taco Company Locations
  - Address
  - Hours
  - Picture of the building
  - Rating (1-5) stars
  - belongs_to :taco_company

Taco Company Location --- belongs to --- a specific taco company
